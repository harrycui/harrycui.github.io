About Me

I am a Research Fellow in the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong (CityU). I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from CityU in October 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Cong WANG. Before that, I obtained my M.Sc. degree in Information Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in November 2013 and B.Eng. degree in Software Engineering from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU) in July 2010.

I will join the School of Computer Science at NWPU as a Tenure-Track Associate Professor in the summer of 2019.

Research Interests

Blockchain and Decentralized Application, Cloud Computing Security, Multimedia Security, Mobile Security, Storage Systems, etc.
